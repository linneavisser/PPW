Silfen

SILFEN is a Danish accessory and clothing brand that seeks to promote individuality and authenticity.
SILFEN approached us looking for a way to effectively communicate their sustainability initiative on their website as well as on their social media channels, where they had seen a drop in engagement for the past months.
