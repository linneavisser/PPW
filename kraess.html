Kræss

Kræss is an independent art & design boutique that sells handmade designs created by local artists. Their core value is to help establish new designers by not only selling their items, but to tell their stories too.
For my final exam project in my second semester I worked in a team to do a full redesign of their current visual profile to better align with their core values, create a more user-friendly website and to help strengthen their digital presence through creating a digital and SoMe marketing strategy.
Some of my responsibilities included creating a new logo, putting together a style guide, copywriting, content ideation and post-production.
